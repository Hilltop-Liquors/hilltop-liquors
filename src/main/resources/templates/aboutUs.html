Welcome to Hilltop Liquors!
Your number one source for beer, wine & spirits. We're dedicated to giving you the very best liquor store in Victoria, Tx with a focus on quality products, dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2019 by the Smith family, Hilltop Liquors has come a long way from its beginnings, starting out with a complete remodel in December 2018 to now being compared with some of the larger corporate run liquor stores! We now serve customers all over Victoria and the surrounding counties and are thrilled to be a part of the very competitive yet rewarding liquor store industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Your local Victoria family, ~The Smiths

Today's: Refresh Recipe:
Malibu Sunset Cocktail

Fill a glass with ice cubes

Pour the pineapple and Malibu Rum over the ice cubes

Pour the grenadine slowly down one side of the glass. *If you pour too quickly, the colors will blend and it won't look as pretty

Garnish with cherries/pineapple if desired. And Serve!
